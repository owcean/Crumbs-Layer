Chocolate Chip
Cookie

A chocolate chip cookie is a classic favorite, with a soft, chewy center studded with gooey chocolate chips. Each bite delivers a perfect blend of sweet, buttery dough and rich, melty chocolate, offering a timeless, comforting treat that never fails to please.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity Notes
All-purpose flour 2¼ cups Sifted
Baking soda 1 tsp Sifted
Salt ½ tsp Kosher
Butter 1 cup Softened
Brown Sugar ¾ cup Packed
White Sugar ¾ cup Granulated
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp Optional
Eggs 2 large Room temperature
Chocolate Chips 2 cups Semi-sweet
